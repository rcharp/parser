What Parsavvy can do for you

Get started in minutes

We can have you up and running and extracting data in just a few minutes.

Our service is designed to take all of the hassle out of getting started so you can spend more time on the things that really matter to your business.

Create custom parsing rules

You're in control. Choose what parts of the email you want to get data from, what specific data you're looking for, and what you want to do with it.

Only want billing info from a recurring email? We can get that for you. Want to remove or fine specific data in the email? We can do that for you too.

Export Data

After you've gotten the data you need from your emails, we'll put it all in a CSV for you and email it to you. That way, you can bring your data wherever you need it.

Capture Important Data

Our parser grabs important content and data from emails that you forward to us, based on your own custom parsing rules.

Once you get the data you need, we'll send it to you in an easy-to-use CSV format.

Multiple Inboxes

Segment your business by having multiple inboxes. That way, you can keep specific emails separate and keep track of all of your emails.
